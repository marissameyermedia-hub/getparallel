4. How Commissions Are Earned

You earn a commission when a new user:

  1. Clicks your unique tracked link (getparallel.vip/r/your-link) or enters your promo code during signup
  2. Creates a Parallel account within 30 days of clicking your link (the attribution window)
  3. Completes a paid subscription to Parallel

Commissions are calculated as a percentage of the first payment amount received from the referred member. Recurring subscription payments do not generate additional commissions.

Commission amounts are recorded when the first payment is confirmed by our payment processor. Commissions are not earned for free trials, canceled orders, or fraudulent transactions.

5. Attribution

Attribution is tracked via:

  • Tracked link: Your unique /r/your-link URL. When someone clicks it, a 30-day attribution cookie is set. If they sign up and pay within that window, you are credited.
  • Promo code: Your unique promo code (e.g., YOURNAME20). If a user enters your code during payment, you are credited regardless of whether they clicked your link.

Each referred user can only be attributed to one affiliate. In the event of competing attributions (both link click and promo code), the promo code takes precedence.

6. Clawback Window

All commissions are subject to a 30-day clawback window from the date of the first payment. During this window, a commission may be reversed ("clawed back") if:

  • The referred member requests a refund
  • The payment is charged back or reversed by the payment processor
  • The referred member's account is found to be fraudulent
  • The subscription is canceled within the applicable refund period

After the 30-day window passes without a reversal, the commission becomes eligible for payout.
